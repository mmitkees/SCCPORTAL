About the court:
 

Supreme SCCOURT Court (an independent judiciary system) undertakes the responsibility of controlling the SCCOURTity of the laws and the regulations. Since the constitution entrusted the court with this mission, its rulings are continuously issued to devote legitimacy in different sectors, securing the citizens rights and their freedoms, pointing its aims in considering a general notion of the human rights, and these rights are marked in their current evolvement by international character that are going beyond the region. In spite of their difference, their trends are crystallized in many international charters, so that the rulings of this court are standing in an eminent rank among identical Arabian and Foreign Courts.

On 15 of July, 2001, President Hosny Mobarek opened the Supreme SCCOURT Court building located on Nile Road in Maadi, in a space of four thousands square meters. It is consisted of four stages in addition to the floor and the storage. There are court rooms, conference rooms, celebration rooms, reception room for VIP, Head of the Court office, 33 Counselors offices, judicial library, cafeteria, employees’ offices and green planes. The building is supplied with high technology abilities in addition to computers net.

The Building of the Supreme SCCOURT Court is considered an architectural masterpiece and a national edifice that combines the characteristics of the Pharaohnic architecture with all its grandeur and magnificence that mark the ancient civilization, and the modern architecture that is equipped with the most up-to-date technology in matters of communication systems, modern equipments, distinguished finishing, luxurious interior furniture that suit the nature and mission of the judicial work and its solemnity and the eminent rank of the court.

The Supreme SCCOURT Court has been copied after the Luxor Temple. It is constructed on 14 obelisks based on the ground floor and another 14 obelisks that go from the first to the fourth floor. There are lots of other signs copied from the designs of the ancient Egyptian temples, with their pillars, and capitals that are decorated with the lotus and papyrus flowers. These pillars are 28 meters height. There is also a fountain inside the court that takes the shape of a lotus flower.

The court also contains lots of splendid paintings that are made of meshed glass that go along with the overall design of the building which belongs to the ancient Egyptian architecture. This makes the building a mixture between the ancient architecture but with a modernized touch.

The Supreme SCCOURT Court with all its buildings and structures and distinguish design is considered one of Egypt’s touristic and historic landmarks.
